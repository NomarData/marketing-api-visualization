About: Health Awareness in the Arab World

Objectives :

Our tool was created to allow users to explore the rich demographic detailed data provided by Facebook Marketing API in order to compare visually and numerically the health-related interests to a baseline luxury-related interests from Arab League countries.

Download the Data:
You can download all the data used in this project here.
How to use:

1) First of all, select the topics of interest you want to compare through the application.

In the figure aside we see the comparison between the number of people who are interested in Obesity Awareness related pages on Facebook compared to people who are interested in Fast Food related pages.

You can toggle the topic selection by clicking on it.

2) This status bar represents a number of people on Facebook who has shown interest in health-related pages (left sided) and luxury-related pages (right sided topics) compared to the total number of Facebook users.

The score in the top is the overall score of the application status. If it is positive, in average people who match the countries and demographic categories selected are more interested in health-related pages. The contrary happens if the score is negative.

The intensity is calculated based on the number of people who likes the selected topic related pages divide by the number of people in Facebook following the current selection of the application.

3) In the map, you can select the countries that will be included in the visualization by clicking on them. You can use the buttons on right to make easy the selection.

The buttons will receive the same color of the countries.

4) The treemaps are a powerful tool where you can filter and explore the data you are visualizing based on gender, age range, scholarity and citizenship.

Click in each treemap's tile to filter the visualization.

The application will dynamically update it's status on the others treemaps, map, and horizontal bar score.

Use case example:

In this case, we are visualizing how people in Golf Arabic region are interested in Health Care and Buy Cars or Vehicles. The treemaps are selected to show people who have graduated, are locals and have age between 25 and 44 years old. We can infer that:

  • Women are much more interested in Health Care than men.
  • Saudi Arabia and Yemen are more interested in Health Care or less interested in buying cars.
